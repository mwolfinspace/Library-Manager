🔍 Search Guide

tag1 tag2 Stories with either tag1 OR tag2
+tag1 +tag2 Stories with BOTH tag1 AND tag2 (required)
-tag1 Exclude stories with tag1
tag1 | tag2 Stories with tag1 OR tag2 (pipe separator)
action +sci-fi -romance Action stories that are sci-fi but NOT romance
tag1, tag2, tag3 Commas are treated as spaces (same as "tag1 tag2 tag3")
